About ChaOz

ChaOz is more than just a pastry shop — it’s a story of passion, faith, and resilience. Born in a small family kitchen in 2021, right in the middle of a global crisis, ChaOz started from the simplest yet most powerful of needs: a mother wanting to bake a birthday cake for her children.

“Life’s trials are stepping stones. They uncover hidden strength within you and guide you toward your destiny.”

At ChaOz, every cake tells a story. From fluffy sponge layers to our signature ChaOz Cream Cake and the reimagined Napolitain, each creation is homemade with care, creativity, and love.

Based at Esplanade du Pouce, Port Louis, ChaOz also offers home delivery to make sure your treats arrive fresh and full of delight, right to your doorstep. ✨
