Welcome to ChaOz

A universe of sweetness, hope, and unforgettable flavors.

ChaOz is more than just a pastry shop. It is a story of passion, faith, and resilience, born in a family kitchen in 2021, at the heart of a global crisis. With no means to buy a birthday cake for her children, the founder of ChaOz taught herself the art of baking. What began as a moment of hardship blossomed into a sweet adventure filled with promise.

At ChaOz, every cake tells a story. From fluffy sponge cakes to custom creations, to our signature ChaOz cream cake and our unique twist on the classic Napolitain — each dessert is lovingly crafted, homemade, and designed to blend taste, beauty, and originality.

Located at Esplanade du Pouce in Port-Louis, ChaOz also offers home delivery, bringing sweetness straight to your door.

“God never abandons you. Challenges are stepping stones. They reveal your hidden strength and guide you toward your destiny.”
